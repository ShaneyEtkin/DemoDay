Homelessness In Our Streets

How Bad is it?

Homelessness is a surprisingly common issue that happens to surprisingly normal people. It's a downwards spiral that most people can't quite escape from, which isn't necessarily their fault. If we help, they could have the leg up they need to get back on track.

What does it look like?

These once every-day people are now people that most avoid even looking at.

This is caused by...

  • Unaffordable housing
  • Poverty
  • Unemployment
  • Displacement
  • Mental illness

They are...

  • Chased from the streets
  • Self medicated
  • Ignored

What Can We Do?

We can participate through the Coalition of Homelessness, by voting on affordable housing props, or getting involved in the community!

Below Are Resources and Ways to Get Involved:

This Coalition holds frequent gatherings and events to take a step towards ending homelessness. Get involved by checking them out!

The SF Chronicle has started a fund to assist homeless individuals in finding houses. You can donate or volunteer on their page.
